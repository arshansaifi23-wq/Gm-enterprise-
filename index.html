About Us

A single team for design, construction and finishing.

GM Enterprise is a Delhi-based interiors, renovation and civil works contractor. We work directly with homeowners, builders and businesses to plan a space, execute the structural work, and finish it to a standard that lasts.

From our base in Sangam Vihar, we run projects across the city — keeping design, labour, materials and timelines under one roof so nothing falls between the cracks.

  • Free on-site consultation
  • In-house masons, carpenters & painters
  • Transparent material-wise costing
  • Fixed timelines with weekly updates
  • Residential & commercial projects
  • Post-handover support
